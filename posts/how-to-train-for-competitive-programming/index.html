Some note for cp training.

Ask yourself some questions:

  • Why did you start doing cp?
  • Does cp bring you any positive or real values? Money? Fame?
  • Should you give up cp now?
  • Why don’t you want to quit cp?
  • What will you gain from cp?
  • Is your time worth it?
